PROJECTS

Neural Machine Translation for Under-resourced Languages (NURS Project)

Sept 2018 - present

I am working on work package 4 (WP4)-Multi-way models for under-resourced scenarios To overcome the data sparsity in under- resourced scenarios, we will engage the idea of combining data of closely related languages to train a single neural network. Since closely related languages share similar vocabulary (cognates) and linguistic features, this knowledge demonstrated to improve the translation quality. Furthermore, since even closely related languages can use different scripts, e.g. Russian and Czech as examples for Slavic languages, these texts have to be transliterated into one common script, to apply the multi-way neural models.

Other creator - Mihael Arcan
Impact of Algorithmic Trading on Capital Market Microstructure

Jan 2015 – Aug 2015

"Impact of Algorithmic Trading on Capital Market Microstructure" from January, 2015 to August, 2015. To analyze historical order data of magnitudes in terabytes. As first step we are simulated Indian Limit order book dynamics. The aim of the project is to construct a framework that captures the dynamics of the limit order book in the Indian stock markets modeled on the set of previous historical data.
